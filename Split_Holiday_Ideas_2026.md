# Split Holiday Ideas — Two-Destination Combos
## Family of 5: 2 Adults, Katie (17), George (15), Tom (8)
## Flying from LBA (preferred) or MAN | ~14-15 days | Summer 2026

Based on what you enjoy (Costa Brava + Dordogne, Milan + Lake Garda,
Croatia island-hopping, Zell am See + Lake Como), you like:
- ✅ Contrasting experiences (mountains/lake, city/beach, culture/nature)
- ✅ Manageable travel between destinations (2-4hrs)
- ✅ Mix of adventure + relaxation
- ✅ Campsite/apartment style accommodation (but enjoy all-inclusives too)
- ✅ Beaches and aqua parks — important for all 3 kids
- ✅ Things for TEENS (Katie 17, George 15) — not just little-kid stuff
- ✅ LBA preferred airport, MAN second choice
- ✅ Family-friendly with things for all ages

## 🏆 TOP PICKS

### 1. 🏔️ Dolomites + 🏖️ Venice/Adriatic Coast (Italy)
**The vibe:** Jaw-dropping mountain scenery → Italian beach culture & canals

| Leg | Duration | What | Transfer |
|-----|----------|------|----------|
| **Dolomites** (Val Gardena/Val di Fassa) | 7 nights | Hiking, via ferrata, cable cars, mountain lakes, alpine coasters | |
| → transfer | | | 3hrs drive or scenic train |
| **Venice + Adriatic** (Jesolo/Caorle/Bibione) | 5 nights | Venice day trip, sandy beaches, water parks, Italian food | |

- **Fly:** MAN → Verona or Innsbruck (in) / Venice (out)
- **Scenic connection:** Drive through stunning Dolomite passes OR train via Bolzano→Venice
- **Campsite options:** Excellent in both! Camping Seiser Alm (Dolomites), Camping Marina di Venezia (beach)
- **Why it works:** Dolomites are the "wow" factor, beach is the wind-down. Venice day trip is a bonus.
- **Budget estimate:** £2,500-4,500

### 2. 🏞️ Lake Annecy + 🌾 Provence (France)
**The vibe:** Crystal-clear Alpine lake → lavender fields, markets, gorges

| Leg | Duration | What | Transfer |
|-----|----------|------|----------|
| **Lake Annecy** | 5 nights | Swimming in Europe's cleanest lake, cycling, old town, paragliding | |
| → transfer | | | 4hrs drive south |
| **Provence** (Gorges du Verdon area) | 7 nights | Gorges du Verdon kayaking, lavender fields, markets, hilltop villages | |

- **Fly:** MAN → Geneva or Lyon (in) / Marseille or Nice (out)
- **Campsite options:** Eurocamp has parks near Annecy AND in Provence/Ardèche
- **Why it works:** Both are quintessentially French but totally different vibes. Verdon gorge = adventure. Tom can swim in both.
- **Budget estimate:** £2,000-3,800

### 3. 🏔️ Bavarian Alps + 🏞️ Lake Bled & Slovenia
**The vibe:** German mountain adventure → fairy-tale glacial lake & caves

| Leg | Duration | What | Transfer |
|-----|----------|------|----------|
| **Bavarian Alps** (Garmisch/Berchtesgaden) | 6 nights | Zugspitze (highest peak in Germany), Eagle's Nest, Königssee lake, alpine coasters, Neuschwanstein Castle | |
| → transfer | | | 3-4hrs drive via Innsbruck |

- **Why it works:** Two countries, similar prices, totally different cultures. Slovenia is incredibly family-friendly and affordable. Postojna Cave + Lake Bled = Tom heaven.

### 4. 🌿 Plitvice Lakes + 🏖️ Istria (Croatia)
**The vibe:** Stunning national park waterfalls → Mediterranean coastal charm

| Leg | Duration | What | Transfer |
|-----|----------|------|----------|
| **Plitvice Lakes area** | 3 nights | Croatia's #1 attraction — cascading turquoise lakes & waterfalls, hiking, nature | |
| → transfer | | | 2.5hrs drive to coast |
| **Istria** (Rovinj/Poreč/Pula) | 9 nights | Beautiful coastal towns, Roman amphitheatre (Pula), beaches, seafood, truffles, Eurocamp sites! | |

- **Fly:** MAN → Zagreb (in) / Pula (out)
- **Campsite options:** Plitvice Holiday Resort (camping.hr), then Eurocamp has **Lanterna** near Poreč — one of their best parks!
- **Why it works:** Plitvice is bucket-list. Istria is Croatia's best for families (less crowded than Dubrovnik). Eurocamp available!
- **Budget estimate:** £2,000-3,500

### 5. 🎨 Cinque Terre + 🏡 Tuscany (Italy)
**The vibe:** Dramatic cliff-side coastal villages → rolling countryside & culture

| Leg | Duration | What | Transfer |
|-----|----------|------|----------|
| **Cinque Terre** (stay in Levanto or Monterosso) | 4 nights | Hike between the 5 colourful villages, swimming, boat trips, seafood | |
| → transfer | | | 2hrs drive/train |
| **Tuscany** (Lucca/San Gimignano area) | 8 nights | Florence day trip, Pisa, hill towns, pools, cycling, cooking, gelato | |

**The vibe:** History & beaches → rugged coast, crêpes & islands

## 💡 MY TOP 3 FOR YOUR FAMILY

1. **Dolomites + Venice/Adriatic Coast** — Mountain wow factor for everyone, then proper sandy beaches + Aquapark Jesolo for the kids. Via ferrata and mountain biking keep Katie & George happy. Jet2 fly LBA→Verona. ✈️
2. **Bavaria + Lake Bled** — Castles, alpine coasters, Zugspitze + fairy-tale lake, Postojna Cave, canyoning in Bled for the teens. Jet2 fly LBA→Munich. Excellent value in Slovenia. ✈️
3. **Plitvice + Istria** — Bucket-list waterfalls then Eurocamp at Lanterna (pools, beach, activities). Aquapark Istralandia nearby. Jet2 fly LBA→Pula direct. Most "all-inclusive feel" with Eurocamp. ✈️

### 🏨 ALL-INCLUSIVE CROSSOVER NOTE:
If you want the split-holiday vibe but with less self-catering hassle, look at:
- **Eurocamp** in Istria or Tuscany (not all-inclusive, but pool complexes + restaurants on site)
- **TUI Family Life** or **Jet2 Family hotels** for the beach leg of any combo
- **Falkensteiner Hotels** in Croatia/Austria — family hotels with pools, kids clubs, half-board
- **Camping resorts with half-board** — Almissa (Croatia) and many Austrian campsites offer HB/FB options
