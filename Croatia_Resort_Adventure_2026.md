## 🎯 DAY TRIPS — CULTURE & ADVENTURE

### 🌊 KRKA NATIONAL PARK (15 min drive!) ⭐ MUST-DO
- Croatia's most family-friendly national park
- **Skradinski Buk** — main waterfall complex, boardwalk trails over cascading water
- **You CAN swim** at the base of the waterfalls!
- Visovac Island — monastery on a tiny island in the river (boat trip)
- Old water mills and traditional buildings
- Entry: ~€15 adults, discounted for children
- **TIP:** Go early (before 10am) or late afternoon to avoid August crowds
- Could easily visit TWICE during 14 nights

### 🏛️ ŠIBENIK OLD TOWN (6km, 10 min drive)
- **Cathedral of St James** — UNESCO World Heritage, entirely built from stone
- St Michael's Fortress — panoramic views, summer concerts
- Barone Fortress — interactive museum with VR experience (teens will love this!)
- Charming narrow streets, local restaurants, live music
- Evening atmosphere is wonderful — go for sunset + dinner

### 🏛️ SPLIT & DIOCLETIAN'S PALACE (1hr drive)
- **Diocletian's Palace** — 1,700-year-old Roman palace, UNESCO, still lived-in!
- Cathedral of St Domnius bell tower — climb for views
- Marjan Hill — hike for panoramic views
- Bačvice Beach — sandy, buzzy, great for teens
- Riva promenade — evening stroll, bars, atmosphere
- **Worth a full day — or even two visits**

### ⚡ CETINA RIVER RAFTING (45 min drive to Omiš) ⭐ TEENS WILL LOVE
- Class II-III rapids through dramatic limestone canyon
- 3-hour guided trip, all equipment provided
- Min age usually 6-8 with parent — **Tom CAN join!**
- Multiple operators in Omiš — book online 2-4 weeks ahead
- **~€35-50pp**

### ⚡ ZIPLINING OVER CETINA CANYON (45 min drive)
- 8 zip lines, longest 700m across the gorge
- Stunning views over the canyon and river
- Min age usually 10-12 — **Katie & George yes, Tom check**
- Book ahead for August
- **~€40-60pp**

### 🏛️ TROGIR (45 min drive)
- UNESCO island town connected by bridges
- Stunning medieval architecture, Romanesque cathedral
- Small, walkable, great for an afternoon/evening stroll
- Good restaurants on the waterfront

### 🏝️ KORNATI ISLANDS BOAT TRIP (from Šibenik/Vodice)
- Croatia's most dramatic island archipelago
- Day trip boat excursions from nearby Vodice or Šibenik
- Swimming stops, lunch on the boat, stunning scenery
- ~€50-80pp including lunch
- **Memorable day for the whole family**

### 🏝️ ISLAND OF ZLARIN (boat from Šibenik)
- Beautiful car-free island, famous for coral
- Easy half-day trip
- Great beaches, peaceful

### 🏖️ PRIMOŠTEN (25 min drive)
- One of the most photogenic towns on the Croatian coast
- Peninsula old town, great beaches nearby
- Beautiful for a half-day trip + beach + dinner

### 🏛️ ZADAR (1.5hr drive)
- **Sea Organ** — waves play music through stone steps
- **Sun Salutation** — solar light installation at sunset
- Hitchcock called this "the most beautiful sunset in the world"
- Roman Forum ruins, old town
- Worth the drive for an evening trip

## 🗒️ PRACTICAL NOTES

- **Currency:** Euro (Croatia adopted € in January 2023)
- **Driving:** Right-hand side. A1 motorway Split–Šibenik is fast (45 min, ~€8 toll)
- **Krka tip:** Arrive before 10am in August to avoid queues
- **Cetina rafting age:** Most operators take age 6-8+. Tom (8) should be fine with a parent.
- **Ziplining age:** Usually 10-12+ minimum. Check for Tom.
- **Amadria Park size:** It's a big resort complex — the campsite is part of a larger park with hotels, the aqua park, marina, and Mediterranean City. You have access to the whole lot.
- **Dalmatian Ethno Village:** A reconstructed traditional village inside the resort with stone houses, old water mill, distillery, olive oil refinery. Great for an evening experience.
- **One base, no packing up** — biggest advantage over the split-resort plan. Unpack once, relax for 2 weeks.
- **This trip stays in ONE country** — no border crossings, one currency, one SIM card. Simple!
