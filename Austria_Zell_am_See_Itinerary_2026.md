## 🚂 SCENIC RAILWAY 3: THE BERNINA EXPRESS (Day 10 — the big one!)

### What is it?
A **UNESCO World Heritage** narrow-gauge railway through the Swiss Alps between
**Chur** (Switzerland) and **Tirano** (Italy). 144km, 55 tunnels, 196 bridges,
climbing to **2,253m** (7,391 feet) at the Bernina Pass. Panoramic sightseeing
cars with huge windows. Arguably the most spectacular train ride in Europe.

**On this trip, the Bernina Express isn't a side trip — it's your journey
FROM Zell am See TO Lake Como!**

### Day 10 Itinerary: Chur → Lake Como

| Time | Leg | Details |
|------|-----|---------|
| 08:17 | **Chur → Tirano** | **🚂 BERNINA EXPRESS PE 951** — 4.5hrs of jaw-dropping scenery |
| 12:49 | Arrive Tirano | Lunch in this Italian town! Pizza, gelato 🍕 |
| 13:08 | Tirano → Varenna-Esino | Regional train, 1.5hrs along Lake Como's eastern shore |
| 14:35 | **Arrive Varenna (Lake Como)** | Your second holiday begins! |

### Highlights along the route:
- **Landwasser Viaduct** — iconic curved viaduct emerging from a tunnel into a cliff face
- **Albula Tunnel** — 6km through the mountain
- **Ospizio Bernina** (2,253m) — the summit, highest rail crossing in the Alps
- **Alp Grüm** (2,091m) — views of the Palü Glacier, pause to take it in
- **Lake Poschiavo** — stunning turquoise mountain lake
- **Brusio Spiral Viaduct** — the train spirals over itself to gain height!
- Then you emerge into sunny Italy at Tirano 🇮🇹

### Costs — THE CLEVER WAY (from Seat61):

There's a legitimate trick: buy a German Railways **Sparpreis** ticket from
**Singen** (Germany) to **Tirano**, and simply don't use the Singen→Zurich part.
This gives you the full Chur→Tirano journey at a fraction of the price!

| Item | 2nd Class | 1st Class |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| **Sparpreis ticket Singen→Tirano** (use from Chur) | **from €47.99 pp** | from €57.99 pp |
| OR Saver Day Pass (unlimited Swiss trains all day) | from CHF 52 (£46) pp | from CHF 88 (£78) pp |
| Bernina Express seat reservation (summer) | CHF 36 (£32) pp | CHF 36 (£32) pp |
| **Children under 15** | **FREE with Sparpreis!** | FREE (still need reservation) |
| Tirano → Varenna regional train | €12.50 pp | €19 pp |

**Tom (8) and George (15) = FREE on the Sparpreis ticket (under 15 rule)!**
They still need a Bernina Express seat reservation at CHF 36 each.

**Estimated Day 10 trains (5 pax, 2nd class, Sparpreis):**
- 3 paying passengers × €47.99 = €144 (2 adults + Katie 17)
- 2 free children (Tom 8 + George 15)
- 5 × seat reservations = 5 × CHF 36 = CHF 180 (£160)
- 5 × Tirano→Varenna = 5 × €12.50 = €62.50
- **Total: ~£360-400**

### How to book the Sparpreis trick:
1. **Seat reservation first** → rhb.ch/en/panoramic-journeys/bernina-express
   (opens ~6 months ahead). Select "reservation only" for each passenger.
2. **Sparpreis ticket** → int.bahn.de
   - From: Singen (Hohentwiel) → To: Tirano
   - Set date to DAY BEFORE your travel date
   - Set departure: 15:00
   - Click "Stopovers" → add Chur with stopover of appropriate length
   - Select the result showing IC + PE 951 (Bernina Express)
   - Kids under 15 are free!
3. **Tirano → Varenna** → buy at trainline.com or at the station (cheap, no booking needed)

### Luggage:
- Take ALL your luggage with you (you're not coming back!)
- Bernina Express has luggage storage areas in each carriage
- Tirano → Varenna regional trains have luggage space

## 🌊 DESTINATION 2: LAKE COMO, ITALY (4 nights, Days 11-14)

### Why Lake Como?
- One of Italy's most beautiful lakes — dramatic mountains plunging into deep blue water
- Charming lakeside villages with colourful houses, narrow lanes, gelato shops
- Regular **ferry/boat services** connect all the villages (fun for the whole family!)
- Swimming in the lake — clean, warm in August (22-24°C)
- Italian food at a fraction of Swiss prices
- Completely different vibe from Zell am See — Mediterranean warmth vs Alpine fresh
- George Clooney lives here (Katie might care!)

### Where to stay: VARENNA ⭐ RECOMMENDED

**Why Varenna over Bellagio or Como town?**
- Arrives direct by train from Tirano (the Bernina Express end point!)
- Quieter and more authentic than touristy Bellagio
- Stunning waterfront with restaurants right on the lake
- Ferry connections to Bellagio (15 min), Menaggio, and Cadenabbia
- Easier departure — direct train to Milan for your flight home
- More affordable than Bellagio

### Accommodation options:

#### OPTION 1: Apartment (Airbnb/Booking.com) ⭐ BEST FOR FAMILIES
- **Search:** Varenna, 7-11 Aug, 2 adults + 3 children
- **What to look for:** 2-3 bedroom apartment with lake views
- **Budget:** €100-180/night = **£350-630 for 4 nights**
- **Also check:** Perledo (village above Varenna, cheaper, car-free)

#### OPTION 2: Hotel in Varenna
- **Hotel du Lac** — right on the waterfront, stunning views
- **Albergo Milano** — family-run, terrace overlooking the lake
- **Hotel Royal Victoria** — grand lakeside hotel with pool
- **Budget:** €150-300/night = **£520-1,050 for 4 nights**

#### OPTION 3: Campsite — Camping Riviera (Lecco)
- **Location:** Southern end of Lake Como, near Lecco
- **Mobile homes & pitches available**
- **Budget:** €60-100/night = **£210-350 for 4 nights**
- **Note:** Further from the prettiest part of the lake

#### ALTERNATIVE VILLAGE: Menaggio
- Western shore, ferry connections, slightly cheaper
- Town beach, lido with pool
- More family-oriented than Varenna
- Also has camping options nearby (Camping Europa)

### Things to do at Lake Como:

#### Whole family:
- **Ferries between villages** — all-day pass available, hop on/hop off
- **Varenna Passarella** — stunning lakefront walkway
- **Villa Monastero, Varenna** — botanical gardens on the lake
- **Villa Melzi, Bellagio** — beautiful gardens and museum
- **Swimming** — public beaches at Varenna, Bellagio, Menaggio
- **Bellagio** — the "Pearl of Lake Como", explore narrow lanes and shops

#### For Katie & George:
- **Kayaking/SUP** on the lake
- **Orrido di Bellano** — dramatic gorge walkway (20 min by ferry from Varenna)
- **Shopping in Bellagio** — leather goods, silk scarves, Italian fashion
- **Water sports** at various beaches

#### For Tom:
- **Ferry boat rides** (he'll love jumping between villages!)
- **Pebble beaches** — skipping stones and swimming
- **Gelato tour** — a different flavour in every village
- **Playground** on the Bellagio waterfront

## 🗒️ PRACTICAL TIPS

- **Currency:** Euro in Austria & Italy, Swiss Franc in Switzerland (€1 ≈ CHF 0.95)
- **Zell am See-Kaprun Card:** Included FREE with most accommodation. Gives free:
  - Cable cars (Schmittenhöhe, Maiskogel, Kitzsteinhorn)
  - Pinzgauer Lokalbahn
  - Local buses
  - Swimming pool & boat trips
  - Many attractions and museums
  **CHECK YOUR ACCOMMODATION INCLUDES IT BEFORE BOOKING**
- **Bernina Express booking trick:** Use int.bahn.de, book Singen→Tirano Sparpreis ticket
  from €47.99pp. Kids under 15 FREE. Don't use the Singen→Zurich part. Perfectly legitimate.
- **Austrian train tips:** ÖBB Vorteilscard Family (€19/year) = kids under 15 FREE with parent
- **Lake Como ferries:** Buy a giornaliero (day pass) for unlimited boat trips between villages
- **Luggage:** Take everything with you on travel days — you're not coming back!
  Bernina Express has luggage storage. Italian regional trains have space too.
- **Weather:** Zell am See 20-28°C, Lake Como 28-32°C (warmer!)
- **Tap water:** Safe to drink everywhere (Austria, Switzerland, Italy)
- **Languages:** German (Austria), German/Italian (Switzerland), Italian (Como)
  — English widely spoken in tourist areas throughout
- **Travel insurance:** Essential for mountain activities
- **Supermarkets:** Spar/Billa/Hofer in Austria, Coop/Migros in Switzerland, Esselunga/Conad in Italy
