The Problem

Planning a family holiday is overwhelming

Hundreds of things to do, scattered across dozens of websites, with no way to filter what actually works for your kids.

🌊

Too Many Options

Every destination has hundreds of activities. TripAdvisor alone returns thousands of results with no structure.

👶

Ages Don't Match

Your 4-year-old and your 12-year-old need completely different things. Most sites don't filter by age.

💸

No Way to Compare

Costs, distances, age limits — all buried in different formats across different websites. No single view.

The Solution

Let AI do the
research for you

What if you could have an AI research every activity at your destination, check age suitability, estimate costs, and build you a beautiful interactive guide — with filters, cost badges, and a QR code to share?

  • 60–90+ activities per destination
  • Filterable by category, age & cost
  • Direct links to book or learn more

01

Set the Scene

Tell Copilot about your family

Start by giving context. Who's travelling? What ages are the kids? What kind of holiday do you want? The more detail, the better the results.

Your prompt
We're a family of 4 — two adults, kids aged 7 and 11. We want a summer holiday in late July, somewhere warm with a mix of beach, adventure, and culture. Budget is mid-range. Can you help us plan?
What Copilot does

Understands your family profile, budget, and preferences. Uses this context for every recommendation that follows.

02

Pick a Destination

Research flights and get suggestions

Ask Copilot to suggest destinations based on your profile, or give it a shortlist. It can research flight options and help you compare.

Your prompt
We're considering the South of France, Croatia, or Sicily. Can you compare them for families with kids aged 7-11? Consider flight times from the UK, weather in late July, and family-friendliness.
What Copilot does

Compares destinations across multiple factors, giving you a structured comparison to make a confident choice.

03

Research Activities

Find 60–90+ activities with costs and links

This is where the magic happens. Ask Copilot to research every activity at your destination — beaches, theme parks, boat trips, cultural sites, rainy-day options, and more.

Your prompt
Research every family activity in the South of France — beaches, water sports, theme parks, cultural sites, boat trips, markets, and rainy-day activities. For each one, include: name, category, estimated cost, age suitability, a short description, and a link. Find at least 80 activities.
What Copilot does

Searches the web comprehensively and compiles a structured list of 80–90+ activities with costs, ages, descriptions, and booking links.

04

Build the Report

Create an interactive HTML report

Ask Copilot to turn all that research into a beautiful, single-page HTML report with category filters, cost badges, and clickable links.

Your prompt
Build a single-page HTML report from these activities. Include: category filter buttons, cost badges (Free / £ / ££ / £££), clickable links, a search bar, and make it fully responsive for mobile. Use a clean, modern design.
What Copilot does

Generates a complete, self-contained HTML file with interactive JavaScript filters, responsive CSS, and all your activity data embedded.

05

Make It Yours

Customise the colours and style

Want it themed to your favourite football team? A specific colour palette? Just ask. Copilot will restyle the entire report to match.

Your prompt
Restyle this report to use a Tottenham Hotspur colour scheme — navy blue and white as primary colours with subtle gold accents. Keep it professional but with a Spurs feel.
What Copilot does

Updates every colour, gradient, and accent across the entire report to match your chosen theme — instantly.

06

Share It

Host online and generate a QR code

Push your report to GitHub Pages and Copilot will generate a QR code you can share with family. Everyone gets the guide on their phone.

Your prompt
Add a QR code to the report that links to its hosted URL. Place it in the footer so people can easily scan it and share.
What Copilot does

Adds a scannable QR code to the page so anyone can pull up the full interactive guide on their phone in seconds.

Try It Yourself

Copy these prompts to get started

Open GitHub Copilot CLI and paste any of these. Adjust the details to match your family.

🎯 Quick Start — Set Your Family Profile
We're a family of 4 — two adults, kids aged 7 and 11. We like beaches, adventure activities, and cultural experiences. Budget is mid-range. Help us plan a week-long holiday.
🔍 Deep Research — Find All Activities
Research every family-friendly activity in [DESTINATION]. Include beaches, water sports, theme parks, cultural sites, boat trips, markets, nature walks, and rainy-day options. For each, provide: name, category, estimated cost per person, minimum age, a one-line description, and a booking/info URL. Aim for at least 80 activities.
📊 Build the Report
Create a single-page HTML report from these activities. Include: interactive filter buttons for each category, colour-coded cost badges (Free / £ / ££ / £££), a text search bar, clickable booking links, and a responsive mobile-friendly layout. Make it look professional and modern.
🚀 The Mega Prompt — Do It All In One Go
I'm planning a family holiday to [DESTINATION] for [NUMBER] people — [DESCRIBE AGES]. We're travelling in [MONTH] and enjoy [INTERESTS e.g. beaches, adventure, culture, food]. Please: 1. Research at least 80 family-friendly activities at [DESTINATION] 2. For each activity include: name, category, cost estimate, age suitability, short description, and a link 3. Build a single-page interactive HTML report with: - Filter buttons for each category - Colour-coded cost badges (Free / £ / ££ / £££) - A search bar - Clickable links to each activity - Responsive design for mobile - A QR code in the footer linking to the hosted page 4. Use a clean, modern design with [COLOUR PREFERENCE] colours 5. Save it as [destination]-activities.html
