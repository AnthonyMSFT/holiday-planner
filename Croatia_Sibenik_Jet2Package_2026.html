🇭🇷 The Jet2holidays Package

The laziest way to do Croatia — and we mean that as a compliment. Jet2holidays flies you direct from Leeds Bradford to Split (2hr 45), transfers you to Amadria Park Šibenik (1hr), and drops you at a resort that has everything: Aquapark Dalmatia (36,000m² of slides and pools), pirate mini-golf, multiple beaches, kids’ clubs, evening entertainment, and half-board dining. You don’t NEED to leave the resort — but with a hire car add-on (or Jet2 excursions), you’ve got Krka waterfalls 15 minutes away, Šibenik old town 6km away, and Split an hour’s drive.

Tom will live in the aquapark. George will be on the zip line and go-kart track. Katie will photograph Šibenik’s Game of Thrones cathedral and the sunset from the Adriatic. Add a day trip to Krka National Park (you can actually swim at the base of the waterfalls) and Cetina River rafting from Omiš, and you’ve got the perfect mix of resort relaxation and Croatian adventure. All without packing and unpacking — unpack once, done.
