🌦️ Warm but expect Alpine storms. August in the Alps means warm, pleasant mornings but frequent afternoon thunderstorms — they're dramatic but usually short. The Kitzsteinhorn glacier (3,029m) will be cold — bring warm layers. Lake Zell is swimmable at 20-22°C. Mountain weather changes fast — always carry a waterproof. Bernina Express and Lake Como will be warmer and sunnier.
