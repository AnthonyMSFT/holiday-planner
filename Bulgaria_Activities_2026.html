🇧🇬 The Holiday at a Glance

The Black Sea coast at a fraction of Mediterranean prices — think long sandy beaches, three water parks, dolphin shows, UNESCO old towns, and jeep safaris through the Balkan mountains. Base yourself in the Sunny Beach–Nessebar area for the best of both worlds: the fun and facilities of a big resort combined with the charm of a 3,000-year-old UNESCO town just minutes away.

This is a brilliant value-for-money family holiday. Tom gets three different aqua parks to choose from. George can go quad biking and paintballing. Katie will love the photogenic cobbled streets of Nessebar and Sozopol. Day trip to Plovdiv — a stunning city with a Roman amphitheatre still hosting events — or explore the fairy-tale Castle of Ravadinovo. All for roughly half what you'd pay on the French Riviera.

☀️ Hot, sunny, and great for the beach. The Black Sea coast in August is reliably warm with long sunny days. Rain is rare and brief. The sea is warm and perfect for families. Inland trips (Plovdiv, Balkan mountains) will be hotter — carry water. Evenings on the coast are pleasantly warm. Very similar conditions to the Mediterranean but significantly cheaper!
