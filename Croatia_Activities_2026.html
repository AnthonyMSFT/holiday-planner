🇭🇷 The Holiday at a Glance

Island-hopping down the Dalmatian Coast — from the medieval walls of Dubrovnik, through the Roman palace of Split, to the cascading waterfalls of Krka and Plitvice. This is a coastline of crystal-clear water, ancient walled towns, and islands you can see from the shore. Travel by ferry, catamaran, and hire car, mixing UNESCO history with adventure and beach days.

Kayak under Dubrovnik's fortress walls at sunset. Raft the Cetina River canyon. Walk through Diocletian's actual palace — where people still live and hang their washing between 2,000-year-old columns. Then cool off at the Amadria Park aquapark, the biggest on the coast. Croatia is that rare destination where history, adventure, and beach holiday collide perfectly.
