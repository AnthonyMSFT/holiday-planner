🇫🇷 The Holiday at a Glance

A two-week adventure split between the wild Gorges du Verdon — Europe's answer to the Grand Canyon — and the glamorous French Riviera. Week one is all about turquoise gorge swimming, white water rafting, paragliding over canyon cliffs, and evenings under the stars in Provence's lavender-scented countryside. Week two shifts to the Côte d'Azur: sandy beaches, boat trips along the red Estérel coast, water parks, and day trips to Monaco, Nice, and Cannes.

This is the kind of holiday where Tom kayaks into a gorge between 300-metre cliffs in the morning, Katie gets her Instagram shot at a lavender field in the afternoon, and George convinces you to try Europe's highest bungee jump. Then you all eat pizza on a terrace overlooking the Mediterranean. It's adventure and relaxation in equal measure — with a soundtrack of cicadas.

☀️ Hot, sunny, and dry. Inland Verdon can hit 34°C — perfect for gorge swimming. The Côte d'Azur enjoys sea breezes. Rain is rare (2-5 days), usually brief afternoon showers. UV is high — pack factor 50. Evenings are warm enough for outdoor dining. The Perseid meteor shower peaks 10-13 Aug — zero light pollution in the Verdon!
