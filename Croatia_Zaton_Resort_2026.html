🇭🇷 The Holiday at a Glance

Zaton Holiday Resort is the resort that other Croatian campsites wish they were. A 5,000m² water park with six slides and heated pools. A 1.5km sandy Blue Flag beach with its own inflatable sea aqua park. Twelve tennis courts, horse riding, diving, archery, and kids’ clubs that run all day. You’re 16km from Zadar (with its famous Sea Organ and Sun Salutation), an hour from Krka’s swimming waterfalls, and two hours from Plitvice Lakes. Stay in a Eurocamp mobile home with air-con and a terrace, and you’ve got the perfect base for two weeks.

Tom will live between the water park slides and the inflatable sea course. George will be on the tennis courts, the go-karts, and pestering you about the diving course. Katie will photograph Zadar’s sunset (Alfred Hitchcock called it “the most beautiful in the world”) and the Kornati Islands. Add day trips to Plitvice, Krka, and Paklenica Canyon, and you’ve got Croatia’s greatest hits — all from one resort. Bookable through Eurocamp, Roan, or direct.
