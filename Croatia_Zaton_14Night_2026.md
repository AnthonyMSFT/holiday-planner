## 🎯 DAY TRIPS — CULTURE & ADVENTURE

### INNER RING (under 30 min drive)

#### 🏛️ ZADAR OLD TOWN (15 min) ⭐ MUST-DO — visit at least TWICE
- **Sea Organ** — the waves play music through stone pipes on the waterfront. Mesmerising.
- **Sun Salutation** — 22m solar-powered light disc, stunning at sunset
- Alfred Hitchcock called Zadar's sunset **"the most beautiful in the world"**
- Walk the ancient city walls (new UNESCO listing!)
- Roman Forum ruins
- Church of St Donatus — 9th century, unique circular design
- Five Wells Square
- Narodni Trg (People's Square) — café culture, live music
- Ice cream on the Riva
- **Go once for daytime sightseeing + once for sunset + evening atmosphere**
- Free to explore (walls walk is ticketed, ~€6)

#### 🏖️ NIN (5 min) — EASIEST DAY TRIP
- Tiny island town connected by stone bridge — incredibly charming
- **Nin salt works** — see how sea salt is harvested (Tom will find this fascinating)
- **Queen's Beach (Kraljičina plaža)** — famous therapeutic mud! Cover yourselves in it.
- Church of the Holy Cross — "smallest cathedral in the world"
- Nin is where Croatian kings were crowned

#### 🏝️ UGLJAN & PAŠMAN ISLANDS (ferry from Zadar, 25 min crossing)
- Car ferry from Zadar to Ugljan — easy half day
- Beautiful quiet islands, olive groves, swimming coves
- Cycle between villages
- Much less touristy than Hvar/Brač

### MIDDLE RING (45 min – 1.5hr drive)

#### 🌿 PAKLENICA NATIONAL PARK (45 min) ⭐ GREAT FOR TEENS
- Dramatic gorge/canyon hiking — Velika Paklenica trail
- **Rock climbing** — one of Europe's best climbing sites (instruction available)
- Manita Peć cave — underground stalactites
- Old WWII bunker (now a museum — George will love this)
- Family-friendly trail options (Velika Paklenica to the first canyon, 1.5hrs)
- Entry ~€8 adults

#### 🏛️ ŠIBENIK + AQUAPARK DALMATIA (1hr) ⭐ PERFECT COMBO DAY
- **Cathedral of St James** — UNESCO, built entirely from stone, no mortar!
- **Barone Fortress** — interactive VR experience (teens love it!)
- St Michael's Fortress — summer concerts, panoramic views
- **Aquapark Dalmatia** at Amadria Park — 36,000m², 300+ water features, lazy river, thrill slides
- **Do: morning in Šibenik Old Town → afternoon at the aquapark**
- Aquapark entry ~€25-35pp

#### 🌿 KRKA NATIONAL PARK (1hr 15min) ⭐ MUST-DO
- **Skradinski Buk** — spectacular cascading waterfalls, boardwalk trails
- **You can swim** at the base of the falls!
- Visovac Island — monastery on a tiny island (boat trip from inside the park)
- Old water mills, traditional buildings
- Entry ~€15 adults, children discounted
- **Arrive before 10am** to avoid August crowds — or go after 4pm
- Easily one of the best days of the holiday

#### 🏝️ KORNATI ISLANDS (boat trip from Zadar) ⭐ MEMORABLE
- Croatia's most dramatic island archipelago — 89 islands!
- Day trip boat excursions depart from Zadar harbour
- Swimming stops at hidden coves, lunch on the boat
- Stark, otherwild landscape — George Bernard Shaw called it "the last day of creation"
- ~€50-80pp including lunch
- Book ahead for August

### OUTER RING (1.5hr+ drive — pick 2-3 of these)

#### 🌿 PLITVICE LAKES NATIONAL PARK (1hr 45min) ⭐ BUCKET LIST
- Croatia's #1 attraction — cascading turquoise lakes connected by waterfalls
- Boardwalk trails through the lake system
- 16 terraced lakes — colours change through the day
- Boat ride across the largest lake
- Full day trip — spend 4-6 hours in the park
- Entry ~€25 adults in peak (children half), includes boat + bus in park
- **Arrive by 9am** — August queues can be brutal
- **This alone is worth the hire car**

#### 🏛️ SPLIT & DIOCLETIAN'S PALACE (2hr drive)
- **Diocletian's Palace** — 1,700-year-old Roman palace, UNESCO
- Cathedral bell tower climb
- Marjan Hill hike — panoramic views
- Bačvice Beach — sandy, buzzy
- Riva promenade
- **Long drive — consider making it an overnight or very early start**

#### ⚡ CETINA RIVER RAFTING + OMIŠ (2hr drive)
- Class II-III rapids, 3hr guided trip
- Pirate fortress in Omiš
- Long drive from Zaton — **combine with Split to make a mega day trip or overnight**
- ~€35-50pp

## 🗒️ PRACTICAL NOTES

- **Zadar airport is 15 min from Zaton** — the easiest airport-to-resort transfer of any option
- **Same airport pick-up/drop-off** — no one-way car hire fees!
- **Ryanair bags:** Remember to pre-book hold bags and seats online — much cheaper than at the airport. 4 bags for family of 5 is enough (kids share). Book 20kg bags.
- **Sandy beach** — Zaton's 1.5km sandy beach is genuinely unusual in Croatia (most beaches are pebble). Kids will prefer this.
- **Eurocamp option:** If booking through Eurocamp, you get their courier service, welcome pack, linen, cots. But it may cost more than booking direct.
- **Plitvice in August:** Timed entry tickets are essential — book online at np-plitvicka-jezera.hr. Arrive by 9am. No swimming allowed here (unlike Krka).
- **Krka vs Plitvice:** Both are waterfall parks. Krka = you can swim, smaller, closer. Plitvice = more dramatic, bigger, no swimming. **Do both!**
- **Paklenica:** Underrated gem. The canyon hike is dramatic and exciting for teens. Not too crowded.
- **Half-board at Zaton:** Buffet-style with good variety. You can upgrade individual meals to à la carte or the konoba for a treat.
- **Zadar twice:** I've put Zadar in twice because the sunset experience alone deserves its own evening visit, separate from daytime sightseeing.
- **Currency:** Euro. Tap water safe. No visa needed. One country, one SIM card.
