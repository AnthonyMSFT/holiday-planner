🇩🇪 The Holiday at a Glance

An Alpine adventure through Bavaria and Salzburg — think fairy-tale castles, crystal-clear lakes, dramatic mountain gorges, and summer toboggan runs. Base yourself near Berchtesgaden for the famous Königssee, Eagle's Nest, and salt mines, then day-trip to Garmisch for the Zugspitze summit (Germany's highest peak), Salzburg for Mozart and Sound of Music, and Neuschwanstein — the castle that inspired Disney.

This holiday is made for families who love the outdoors. George will want to ride every Alpine coaster in sight, Tom will be mesmerised by the electric boat gliding silently across the emerald Königssee, and Katie will fill her camera roll at Neuschwanstein. Expect hearty Bavarian food, beer gardens (soft drinks for the kids!), and some of the most breathtaking scenery in Europe.
