🇪🇸 The Holiday at a Glance

From Gaudí's Barcelona to the white villages of Andalusia, Spain covers every kind of family holiday in one country. Choose your coast: the Costa Brava's rocky coves and water parks near Barcelona, the Costa Dorada's PortAventura and Ferrari Land, Benidorm's Aqualandia on the Costa Blanca, or the Costa del Sol's cliffside walkways and Moorish palaces. Plus day trips to the Alhambra, Seville, and the dramatic gorge town of Ronda.

George will campaign hard for Ferrari Land (Europe's tallest vertical accelerator coaster). Tom will be in water-park heaven with Aqualandia, Water World, and Caribe Aquatic Park. Katie will photograph the Sagrada Familia from every angle. Walk the Caminito del Rey — a jaw-dropping pathway pinned to a cliff face — then cool off at Nerja's stunning caves. Tapas crawls, paella cooking classes, and flamenco round off the perfect Spanish adventure.

☀️ Hot, sunny, and reliably dry. Spain in August is classic summer — long sunny days, warm seas, minimal rain. Barcelona and Costa Brava are slightly cooler (28-30°C) than the Costa del Sol and inland Andalusia (32-38°C). The Alhambra and Seville will be scorching — visit early morning. Beach conditions are perfect. All coastal areas have warm, swimmable seas. Peak tourist season — book everything in advance.
