🇭🇷🇲🇪 Croatia & Montenegro: Dubrovnik to the Adriatic Riviera

✈️ Fly in: LBA/MAN → Dubrovnik (Jet2 direct, 2hr45). Fly home: Tivat → MAN (Jet2/easyJet direct, 3hr15). Open-jaw = no backtracking!
🚗 Dubrovnik → Kotor: 2hrs (inc. border crossing). Kotor → Budva: 40min. Budva → Tivat Airport: 10min!

🇭🇷🇲🇪 The Holiday at a Glance

The ultimate Adriatic road trip — fly into Dubrovnik’s medieval fortress city, explore Game of Thrones filming locations and kayak under the city walls. Then drive across the border into Montenegro’s Bay of Kotor — a fjord-like inlet ringed by mountains, medieval towns, and tiny island churches. Finish on the Budva Riviera’s golden beaches and the iconic island of Sveti Stefan. Fly home from Tivat — just 10 minutes from your hotel. Two countries, one currency (Euro!), zero backtracking.

George will be buzzing after kayaking under Dubrovnik’s fortress walls and the Blue Cave speedboat tour. Tom will love the boat ride to Our Lady of the Rocks and the beaches of Jaz and Mogren. Katie will fill her phone at Sveti Stefan (Montenegro’s most photographed spot), Kotor’s cat-filled alleys, and Dubrovnik’s sunset from Srđ. And the Tara River Canyon — Europe’s deepest — offers world-class rafting if you fancy a day trip inland. This holiday ticks history, beaches, adventure, and two countries in under two weeks.

☀️ Hot, sunny, and perfect for the Adriatic. August is peak summer — blazing sunshine, warm seas (25-26°C), and very little rain. Any showers are brief. Both Dubrovnik and the Montenegro coast share the same Mediterranean climate. UV is very high — factor 50 essential. Evenings are warm enough for outdoor dining in t-shirts. It’s peak season so book everything in advance — especially Dubrovnik city walls (morning slots sell out) and Kotor boat tours.
